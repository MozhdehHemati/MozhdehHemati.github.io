About

I am currently a PhD researcher at Monash University, working on optimization and market design for consumer energy resources. My research focuses on electricity markets, ancillary services, pricing mechanisms, and optimization-based decision-making for energy systems.

Research Interests

  • Distributed energy resources and smart energy systems
  • Electricity markets and ancillary services
  • Market design and pricing mechanisms
  • Optimization and decision-making for energy systems
  • Energy data analysis and market modelling
